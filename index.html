About Us

Our Mission: Grace & Green is dedicated to creating premium jute products that protect our planet while empowering rural artisans and communities. We believe sustainable business can drive positive change.

Our Vision: To be the leading voice in sustainable jute manufacturing, proving that eco-friendly products can be both beautiful and profitable while supporting traditional craftsmanship.

Our Commitment: Every Grace & Green product represents our dedication to fair trade practices, zero plastic packaging, and supporting sustainable livelihoods in jute-growing communities across Bangladesh and India.
